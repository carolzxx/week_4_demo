The Bank of Canada aims to be accessible to everyone, guiding its Reconciliation journey and promoting economic stability and financial well-being. It supervises payment service providers and ensures risk management requirements are met to keep funds safe. The bank also publishes research papers, public consultations, and surveys related to bank notes, and has started designing a new $20 bank note featuring King Charles III. The bank's economic growth forecasts are influenced by interest rates, tariffs, and inflation, with a focus on maintaining a 2% inflation rate.
